2018 Bunny Census Frequently Asked Questions




What is this bunny census, anyway?

The 2018 Bunny Census is a way for of us to map the cutest areas in Britain. We want to know about all bunnies, pet, plush toy, wild, even imaginary.


Who has to take part?

Participation in the 2018 Bunny Census is voluntary. After all, you can't force cuteness.


And what's the point of all this?

We are mapping cuteness for a better Britain. And practising web design at the same time.


Where can I see the results?

Results will be available via the 'Stats' button below when enough data has been collected/the makers of the website have figured out how to collate the stats.


What about cats/dogs/adorable lizards?

We are currently only collecting data about bunnies. If you would like to pay us to create a cat census website, please get in touch here.
